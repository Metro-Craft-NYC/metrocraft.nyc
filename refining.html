refining

The process of creating can take time and many iterations. On that journey it is important to get feedback from others, in order for us to gain new perspectives or points of reference. Healthy critiques, reviews, and demonstrations all help us improve our process and final product.

At metro craft you have a safe place to privately review and critique your work with your peers. No more pinning up on your apartment walls after a dinner with friends to get that valuable feedback needed for the next stage of your project.

In a focused setting only dedicated to the work being presented, we can keep our attention and the discussion on what matters. There aren't many spaces in the city to be able to gather privately around arts or crafts and have such a discussion. Metro craft is here to change that.
