sharing

From first time presenter to seasoned veteran, we all grow when we take the opportunity to share our work with others. It gives us a moment for feedback, healthy vulnerability and most importantly the satisfaction of a job well done.

At metro craft we hold space for all kinds of artists, craftsfolk, or just dedicated individuals who want to share with their friends and family the amazing work that keeps them up at night.

By sharing what we do, we can also inspire others to continue their own pursuits and demonstrate how easy and enriching it is to present work that has been living privately in our hearts to the public.
