teaching

There is a saying we value at metro craft: each one, teach one. It embodies this idea that if we learn something ourselves, it is important for us to pass it on! This is how we can ensure the future is bright and filled with equipped individuals.

We also think teaching is more commonplace than one may think. It is not just for those with degrees, but for anyone who is so fascinated by a subject they can't help but share it with others.

Fostering a community of self-teaching, exploration, and curiosity is critical to growing our community. There are probably a few topics you yourself could teach already, and you'd be surprised how many want to know what you know.
