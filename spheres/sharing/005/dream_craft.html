Dream Craft mural wall is a collaboration between the MetroCraft community arts space and the Brooklyn Emerging Mural Artists Coalition.
🎨 Ten artists were tasked with envisioning their interpretation of the words “Dream” and “Craft” to create the series of designs seen on the wall.
🎗️ This event will feature a mural walk, where each artist will briefly speak on their contribution to the wall and answer a few questions about their design and process.
🪨 Children and families were also invited to contribute to the community painting of the giant rock near the wall!
